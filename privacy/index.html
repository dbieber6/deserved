Privacy Policy

Deserved · Effective July 4, 2026 · Last updated July 4, 2026

Short version: Deserved keeps the information you enter on your own device. It does not collect your personal data, does not send it to the developer or any third party, and contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking.

This policy explains how the Deserved iPhone app (“Deserved,” “the app,” “we,” or “us”) handles your information.

Information you create

Deserved lets you track actions, categories, rewards, points, reminders, and related items. This information is created by you and stored locally on your device using Apple’s Core Data framework. We do not have access to it and it is not transmitted to us or to any third-party server.

iCloud sync

If iCloud sync is enabled and you are signed in to iCloud, your Deserved data may be synced across your own devices through Apple’s iCloud (CloudKit private database). In that case the data is stored in your personal iCloud account, is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy, and remains inaccessible to the developer. You can turn iCloud off for Deserved in the iOS Settings app.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Deserved schedules local notifications on your device. These are generated on-device and are not routed through any external push server operated by us.

Widgets

Deserved’s home-screen widgets read your data from a private, on-device app group container so they can display your current information. No data leaves your device as a result.

Purchases and subscriptions

Any purchases or subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. We never see or receive your payment details. Apple handles billing and may provide us with limited, non-identifying transaction status so the app can unlock purchased features. You can view, manage, or cancel a subscription at any time in the App Store settings on your device. Purchases are also governed by Apple’s terms.

Analytics and tracking

Deserved contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software. It does not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) and does not track you across apps or websites. We do not sell or share your information.

Children’s privacy

Deserved is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. Because the app does not collect personal data, none is gathered from children either.

Data retention and deletion

Because your data lives on your device, you are in control of it. You can delete individual items within the app, or remove all data by deleting the app from your device. If iCloud sync was enabled, you can also remove the app’s data from iCloud in the iOS Settings app.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will post the updated version on this page and revise the “Last updated” date above.
