How does Deserved work?

Create actions (one-time tasks or repeating habits) and decide how many points each is worth. Check them off to earn points, then spend your balance on rewards you define — a movie night, takeout, a weekend getaway. The app keeps a full ledger of every point you earn and spend.

How do I restore my Premium subscription?

Open Settings in the app and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple Account you used to subscribe.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

In the app, go to Settings → Manage Subscription, or open the iOS Settings app and go to your Apple Account → Subscriptions. Your data is never affected by cancelling — everything you've created stays on your device.

How do I fix a mistake in my balance?

Tap a completed action to un-check it, or use Settings → Adjust Balance… to add or subtract points manually. Purchases can be refunded by swiping on them in your history.

How do I erase my data?

Use Settings → Reset All Data… to permanently delete everything, or simply delete the app. Your data lives only on your device — see the privacy policy.

Widgets aren't updating — what do I check?

Widgets refresh when you open the app and periodically in the background. If a widget looks stale, open Deserved once and it will republish the latest data. To add a widget, long-press your home screen, tap the +, and search for Deserved.
